  Case Study: Brexify 
  Solving the Reliability Problem | I built Brexify, a financial-correctness harness for Brex spend that scores every transaction for fraud, replays policy, and tie-out-verifies every number — so Brex can scale autonomous finance agents without the risk of approving a fraudulent charge. | Link:  brexify.up.railway.app  
  By enforcing spend policy and accounting identities at the decision layer, the harness catches fraud, duplicate invoices, and policy violations before money moves — the integrity Brex needs to let agents act on real dollars.

  Structural Checks | Three-way match · duplicate & bank-change detection
